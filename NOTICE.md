## Purpose and Scope

This archive exists to support:

1. Personal study of CS 61A material by the repository owner
2. Production of Korean-language lecture dubs (see `pipeline/`)
3. A stable offline reference when the live course URL has rolled over
   to a later semester

It is **not** intended to substitute for the official course, and no
claim of ownership over UC Berkeley course content is made or implied.
Users enrolled in CS 61A at UC Berkeley should follow their current
semester's official policies and submit only their own work.

## Takedown Policy

If you are a copyright holder and object to any material included here,
please open an issue at
<https://github.com/NoelClay/ucb_cs61a_fall2022/issues> or contact the
repository owner directly. The disputed material will be removed
promptly — typically within 7 days of verified request.

No adversarial stance is intended: this repository will comply
immediately with any good-faith takedown request from UC Berkeley, the
CS 61A teaching staff, or any other rights holder.
